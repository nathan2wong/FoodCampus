Sign up to deliver!

1. Identify when you intend on ordering take-out.
2. Identify where you intend on going to order take-out.
3. Identify where you will be after ordering (e.g. Soda Hall).
4. Other students place their orders with you for a small commission.
5. The student purchaser will meet you at your final destination to pick up their order.

Arrange your next meal!

1. Look through students listings of where they intend to eat.
2. If you see somewhere you like, order something for yourself too!
3. Meet the student courier at their final destination to pick up your food.
4. Once you have picked up your food, the student courier will be paid.
